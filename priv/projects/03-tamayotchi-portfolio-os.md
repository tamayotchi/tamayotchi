Tamayotchi is a personal Phoenix-powered space for investments, writing, experiments, and private portfolio telemetry.

* Password-gated financial sections keep sensitive balances private while preserving a public homepage.
* Provider detail pages, salary counter, blog publishing, and static SPA support live in one cohesive interface.
* Designed with a cyber-terminal visual language, responsive layouts, and subtle interaction details.
