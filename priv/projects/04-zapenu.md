Zapenu is a menu-commerce platform that lets hospitality brands launch digital menus, capture orders, and control their guest experience from one place.

* Composed of three tightly integrated apps: a back-office console for brands, a multi-tenant API for catalog and orders, and a customer-facing menu with adaptive UI.
* Supports omnichannel ordering including dine-in, pickup, delivery, and WhatsApp ordering flows with payment integration baked in.
* Features a drag-and-drop menu builder with multi-image uploads, option builders, tiered pricing, and live preview tools.
* Generates QR codes per table so guests can scan, browse, and order directly from their phones.
* Uses Cloudflare Workers and smart caching for edge performance, Supabase for authentication and data, and Mercado Pago for payment processing.
