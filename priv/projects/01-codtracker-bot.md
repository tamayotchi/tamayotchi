CodTracker Bot is a Discord bot for tracking Call of Duty Warzone performance and showing player stats directly inside chat.

* Registers Discord users with their Call of Duty username so players can request their own stats with a simple command.
* Fetches Warzone data from the Call of Duty API and renders wins, global K/D, time played, games played, daily K/D, and kills.
* Presents results as Discord embeds so the stats are easy to scan while staying in the server conversation.
