Prezio is an Elixir-powered web experience for tracking product prices across stores and making faster buying decisions.

* Captures live product listings with best-price badges, store links, update timestamps, and comparable offers.
* Keeps the interface simple: products are easy to scan and external purchase links remain one click away.
* Built with Elixir and runs as a dedicated public product at prezio.tamayotchi.com.
